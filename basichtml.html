Recipe #1
Burgers

This is a recipe to make delicious burgers.
The ingredients that we need to prepare the burger are potato, Tomato and cheese.
Cheese is not mandatory but we can use it to make cheese burgers.

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity Mandatory
potato 1kg yes
tomato 1kg yes
cheese 250gm no

Step 1: Toast the buns thoroughly so that they turn golden brown.
Step 2 : Make patties using the potatoes available. We must ensure that the patties are not too hard and not too soft.
Step 3: put the patties between the bun and roast for some time.
Step 4: Serve the burgers hot and spicy.
